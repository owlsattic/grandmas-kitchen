How Smart-Parlour works

Six simple steps that quietly organise your kitchen, reduce waste, and put money back in your pocket.

1. Smart-Parlour learns your kitchen

Add items as you unpack shopping, scan receipts, or update as you go.

2. It keeps everything up to date

Fridge, freezer and cupboards all stored in one simple, calm view.

3. You get gentle expiry alerts

Smart-Parlour nudges you before food goes off, so more of what you buy gets eaten.

4. It shows you what you can cook

Tap once to see meal ideas based on what you already have at home.

5. Smart shopping lists & deals

Build lists from low-stock items and missing ingredients, and keep an eye on better-value options.

6. Quick-Chef™ plans full meals

Tell Quick-Chef how many meals you need and any dietary requests, and it creates simple, step-by-step guides.

Questions & answers

Do I need to be “techy” to use Smart-Parlour?

No. If you can tick items off a shopping list, you can use Smart-Parlour.

How does it actually save me money?

By helping you use what you already have before it goes off, avoiding duplicate buys, and planning meals around existing ingredients.

Can I cancel my plan?

Yes. You can cancel any time. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Will there be new features?

Yes. Smart-Parlour will grow over time. Monthly and yearly members get access to improvements as they are released.
